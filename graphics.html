Rad-Thrash

Illustrator, 2022 Print Ad for a small skate-shop (Rad-Thrash) in South Lake Tahoe, CA

Graphic print ad made for Rad-Thrash features bright colors juxtaposed with a black and white, textured cut out for some interest. The inspiration behind this piece came from the clients want for something colorful, yet not too loud, and fun.
